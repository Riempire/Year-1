Website Use

This website's function is to inform users who are curious about information on keyboards and typing as a whole. This includes history, inner mechanics, and progression.

Hansen Writing Ball

The Hansen Writing Ball was one of the first typewriter-like devices invented. It was the first commercially sold writing device.

Sholes and Glidden Typewriter

This was the first typewriter that used the QWERTY keyboard layout and was commercially sold. It could only print upper-case letters.

Model M Keyboard

These are a group of keyboards made by IBM starting in 1985, and later by various other companies. They used a buckling-spring mechanism to operate.
